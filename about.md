# About This Blog

Welcome to my personal blog where I share thoughts on web development, technology, and digital life. This blog is built with Jekyll and features seamless newsletter integration powered by Buttondown.

## What You'll Find Here

- **Web Development**: Tips, tutorials, and insights about modern web development
- **Technology**: Reviews and thoughts on the latest tech trends
- **Personal Projects**: Showcasing interesting projects and experiments
- **Industry Insights**: Commentary on the tech industry and its evolution

## Newsletter

I publish a weekly newsletter featuring:
- Curated web development resources
- Personal insights and learnings
- Project updates and announcements
- Industry news and analysis

The newsletter is powered by [Buttondown](https://buttondown.email), a privacy-focused email platform that respects your inbox and your data.
